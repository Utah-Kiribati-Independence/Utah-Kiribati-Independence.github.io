A celebration of home, held in Utah

Kiribati marks its independence each July. In 2027, our community in Utah is coming together to celebrate it here — a weekend of culture, food, dance, and connection for families and friends.

🌺

Culture

Te mwaie dance, song, and the traditions of the islands — shared with the next generation and our neighbors.

🤝

Community

A gathering point for I-Kiribati families across Utah and beyond — a botaki to reconnect and belong.

🎉

Independence

Honoring the freedom and heritage of Kiribati with a flag raising, an anthem, and pride in who we are.
